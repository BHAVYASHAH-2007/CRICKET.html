SOME BASIC INFORMATION ABOUT CRICKET

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players.
The main objective is to score more runs than the opposing team.
Matches are played on an oval-shaped field with a rectangular pitch in the center.
Players utilize a bat and ball to score runs, with various ways to get the batsmen out.
